Fluffy Quinoa

Home Quinoa White Rice Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or veggie broth
  • 1/4 tsp salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water to remove bitterness.
  2. Bring water or broth to a boil in a pot.
  3. Add quinoa and salt, then reduce heat to low.
  4. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until water is absorbed.
  5. Fluff with a fork. Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes and serve.
