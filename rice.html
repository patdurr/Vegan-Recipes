Perfect White Rice

Home Quinoa White Rice Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine or basmati white rice
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Bring water and salt to a boil.
  3. Add rice, reduce heat to low, and cover tightly.
  4. Simmer for 18 minutes, then turn off heat and let steam for 5 more minutes.
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve.
