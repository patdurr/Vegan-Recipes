Spicy Tofu Scramble

Home Quinoa White Rice Tofu Scramble

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: grilled potatoe, chopped bell pepper, onion, spinach/kale

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Break tofu into crumbles and add to the pan.
  3. Cook for 3â€“5 minutes until slightly golden.
  4. Stir in turmeric, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and nutritional yeast.
  5. Add veggies if using, and cook until tender.
  6. Season to taste and serve warm.
